Our Story

Satori Holidays is a travel company set up with a motive to help people explore the world with many exciting, exclusive and pocket friendly offers. During our college days we are taught Work to Live But today, we have forgotten to live amidst that work. Every single human is just so exhausted and stressed day in and out, right from a school going child to a working professional. This stress is becoming a major cause for increasing diseases, mental disorders and of course a hindrance for peace of mind.

What can be the best stress buster for these human machines?
The answer we got was 3 E's of nature
1) Explore
2) Enjoy
3) Experience

We at Satori Holidays, decided to help relieve this stress and cater to the society by choosing the best stress buster i.e. the three E's. Every place will tell a different story, a story that will be worth Exploring. Every place will give a different excitement, an excitment worth Enjoying. And every place will give its own vibe, a vibe worth Experiencing.
We are fairly young, receptive to new ideas, flexible and adaptable to our client's need . We look forward to help and cater the best services with the best prices specially for the ladies who rarely get opportunity to breathe freely and practice the 3 E's.

OUR MAIN MOTIVE
1) To make your trip worth a beautiful memory with optimum services.
2) To suffice everyone by offering the best and pocket friendly offers.
3) To help everyone practice the concept of the three E's with us only at Satori holidays.
